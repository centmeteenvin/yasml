# Security Policy

## Supported versions

| Version | Supported |
| ------- | --------- |
| 0.2.x   | Yes       |
| < 0.2   | No        |

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public issues.**

Instead, use [GitHub's private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/centmeteenvin/yasml/security/advisories/new)
to submit a report. This keeps the details confidential until a fix is available.

In your report, please include:

- A description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue or a proof-of-concept.
- The potential impact (what an attacker could achieve).
- The yasml version(s) affected.

## What to expect

- **Acknowledgement** — you will receive a response within **7 days**
  confirming that the report was received.
- **Assessment** — we will investigate and keep you informed of our progress.
- **Fix & release** — once a fix is ready, a new patch version will be
  published and the advisory will be made public.

## Disclosure policy

We follow **coordinated disclosure**. We ask that you:

1. Allow a reasonable amount of time for us to address the issue before
   making any information public.
2. Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond what is necessary to demonstrate it.

We appreciate your help in keeping yasml and its users safe.
